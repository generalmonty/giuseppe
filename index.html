The Giuseppe Spritz

is the Italicus-born cousin of the Hugo—light, floral, and unmistakably Mediterranean. Rumor places its origin along the French Riviera, though it is enjoyed by tastemakers throughout Italy and the broader seaside realm. Often shortened to simply “a Giuseppe,” this bright, spiritous sip is built for warm air and unhurried time. The perfect companion to an endless golden hour.

Giuseppe Spritz

  • 1 part Italicus
  • 2 parts Prosecco
  • Squeeze of lemon
  • Splash of soda
  • Garnish with a lemon and basil
  • Serve over ice

Variations

  • Giuseppe Rio — use Brazilian Espumante and mint in place of Prosecco and basil.
  • Giuseppe Crete — keep the classic build, but garnish with olives.
